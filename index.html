About

Building AI that holds
under attack

I'm an AI Engineer with a focus on secure, adversarially robust machine learning for mission-critical environments. Currently at GVR Technolabs, I work on DRDO (Ministry of Defence) projects involving deep model security assessments and attack-resilient computer vision systems.

My background spans the full ML lifecycle — from custom dataset collection on highway camera feeds and model training, to edge deployment on NVIDIA Jetson devices and live production dashboards. I care deeply about AI that behaves predictably even when adversaries try to break it.

Previously at VaaaN Infra, I developed real-time YOLO-based vehicle detection and incident monitoring systems deployed across NHAI/MoRTH smart highway infrastructure. I'm now expanding into Generative AI and LLM security evaluation, including safety testing of Hugging Face models and locally deployed LLMs.
